## Overview

Cursed Crown is an anime-style action RPG featuring dynamic combat, deep exploration, and relationship-driven gameplay. Discover ancient ruins, wield weapons with customizable elemental skills, and forge bonds with lifelike AI characters to restore peace to the fantasy world of Alder.

## What I Built

- Dynamic Weapon and Skill Tree System: Each weapon you discover comes with a randomly generated set of elemental ability trees, offering endless possibilities and customization. Acquire powerful weapons by completing quests, exploring re-playable maps, and building deep connections with NPCs.

- Relationship-Driven Gameplay: Engage with diverse AI-driven NPCs, each possessing distinct personalities, roles, and questlines. Develop relationships through conversations, errands, and story-driven quests. Strengthen these bonds to unlock exclusive rewards, including rare items, unique weapons, and cosmetic enhancements.

- Rich Exploration and Combat: Venture into intricately designed ruins and immersive landscapes filled with challenging enemies and hidden secrets. Test your skills in fluid, action-packed combat enhanced by your personalized arsenal and abilities.
